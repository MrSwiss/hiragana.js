hiragana.js

A simple web-based IME

Type some Japanese (hiragana only) in the area below! Try "daisuki anata" if you can't think of something to say.

Want this on your site? It's open-source. Check out how easy it is to use:

<input type="text" data-ime="hiragana" />
<script type="text/javascript">ime.enable()</script>
